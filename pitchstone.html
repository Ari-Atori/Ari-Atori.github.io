PITCHSTONE EDITOR

Pitchstone Editor is a current project of mine that was originally developed in OpenGL and GLFW, but is ultimately being transitioned over to Vulkan and SDL2.

Pitchstone is a modular, node-tree based video editor which allows for the use of custom primitive shaders and render jobs for videos.
